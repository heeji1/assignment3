Nespresso Inissia Espresso Machine by De'Longhi

The Nespresso Inissia delivers impeccable single-serve coffee or espresso cups every time. Thanks to its automatic operation and patented extraction system, it can generate up to 19 bars of pressure for that perfect brew. Choose between Espresso for a classic, intense shot, or Lungo for a longer coffee with a milder flavor.


The Inissia is designed with efficiency in mind. Its compact size makes it ideal for smaller spaces. Worried about leaving the machine on? Don’t be! The Inissia automatically switches off after 9 minutes of inactivity, conserving energy and ensuring safety. Plus, its sleek design complements various interior styles.

  • Weight: 5.29 pounds
  • Dimensions: 4.7 x 12.6 x 9 inches
  • Water Tank Capacity: 24 ounces
  • Used Capsule Capacity: 10 capsules
  • Heat Up Time: 25 seconds
  • Capsule Type: Nespresso Original
  • Warranty: One year
